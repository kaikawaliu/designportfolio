Digital Banking 

SeaBank is an digital banking app, which aims to better the lives of individuals in Southeast Asia by making financial services accessible through technology. With an automated onboarding process and user-friendly interface, our services allow customers to enjoy seamless banking transactions on their mobile phone anytime and anywhere.

1. Mari Invest

Mari Invest is a wealth product with the objective of getting customers started on investments through intuitive buying and selling user journeys.

Mari Invest's first offering is low-risk Unit Trust to generate better returns on idle cash. By offering a product with no advisory, the objective is for the investment journey to be as straightforward and simple as possible.

Business Requirement

Create a simple invest product that generally understood by retail investors, which include initial product scopes includes: Onboarding KYC, Buy and Sell fund, Supported Payment Methods, View Fund Details, Transaction History, E-statement.

  

Target Audience

To target a broader user base than the mutual fund marketplace (plan to launch in the next phase), users can put their money here rather than traditional banks with similar flexibility but higher return.

Product Definition

MMF-Money Market Fund, a kind of mutual fund that invests in highly liquid, near-term instruments.  These instruments include cash, cash equivalent securities, and high-credit-rating, debt-based securities with a short-term maturity (such as U.S. Treasuries).  Money market funds are intended to offer investors high liquidity with a very low level of risk.

  

Competitor Study

Several competitor studies were done in order to compare similar investment products in the market and understand better on our product, due to confidential agreement, contents have been modified.

User Journey

Break down the user journey into five stages: Trigger, Awareness, Consideration, Conversion and Advocacy & Repeat Behaviour.

User Interview & Testing

Both quantitative research and qualitative research were done to prove hypothesis, understand user needs and improve user experience.
