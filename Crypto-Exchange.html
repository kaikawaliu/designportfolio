PROJECT

OKX
Crypto Exchange

Onboarding

Overview

OKX is a global cryptocurrency spot and derivatives exchange and the second biggest crypto exchange by trading volume, serving over 50 million people globally. OKX announced the establishment of its Hong Kong entity (OKX Hong Kong) in March 2023 for the purpose of applying for the VASP licence and operating as a virtual asset trading platform in Hong Kong.

My Role

I am the product designer for the user acquisition product line, responsible for related UX/UI Design work, User Research, and Design Standards.

Business Requirement

Optimise the onboarding journey to engage more users & encourage them to take actions, improve click through rate and first time deposit rate.

Target Audience

New OKX users who sign up or intend to sign up, from either referral channal or organic traffic.

Competitor Study

Several competitor studies were done in order to compare similar crypto products in the market and understand better on our product, due to confidential agreement, more details will be presented during interview session.


User Journey

Break down the user journey into five stages: Trigger, Awareness, Consideration, Conversion and Advocacy & Repeat Behaviour.  More details will be presented during interview session.

User Interview & Testing

Both quantitative research and qualitative research were done to prove hypothesis, understand user needs and improve user experience. More details will be presented during interview session.

Design Preview

Reward Centre

Overview

After registering with OKX, users will be eligible to receive the New User Bonus in the Reward center consisting of multiple mystery boxes, they are able to collect, redeem and view rewards in Reward Centre.

Referral

Overview 

Encourage users' friends to create an OKX account with their referral code, and both of them will be eligible to receive Mystery Boxes when referee complete a set of simple tasks.
