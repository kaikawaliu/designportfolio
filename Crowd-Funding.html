PROJECT

Decentralized Crowdfunding Platform

Crowdfunding

Overview

This project is a decentralised kickstarter where user can back their favorite IP projects via IP tokens or NFTs, while NFT stands for the exclusive content or the licensing for commercial usage right of the content, which is empowered by the "Can't Be Evil" NFT Licensing Framework by A16Z crypto labs.

My Role

I am the product designer for the entire website, responsible for related UX/UI Design works and overall Design Standards.

Problem / Challenge

Donate And Wait

CCompare with traditional crowdfunding platform's workflow, existing user have to lock their fund until the product finally delivered, leads to low liquidity and highly uncertainty for long-term projects.

High Barrier Of Trust

NFT investing experience is too risky for those who are not professional investors, and not enough risk control to secure users' fund. Retail investors' confidence are draining out with all the irresponsible conductor in the industry.

Lacking Of Transparency

There is always an absolute power to refuse, cancel, terminate or delete projects during its crowdfunding, such centralised policy may lead to misconduction or bias from the team which strongly discourages innovation.

Project Overview

Break down the project into phases, and plan the timeline for MVP1 demo first to attend ETHGlobal.

Design Language System

Library which includes fundamental elements such as typographic, colour, icons, and various components

User Interview

At UX design stage, qualitative research was done to prove hypothesis, understand user needs.

Findings

Donate And Wait

Users are research oriented, and want more resources directly on the webpage. They want to see white paper, tokenomics, high level roadmaps, vesting schedules, and links to socials all directly from a project's page without digging through Telegram and Medium.

Locate & Preview Pools

Users complain about the size of project previews on the IGO launchpad tab because it creates a long scroll. They want to quickly search for projects they may have research elsewhere

Lacking Of Transparency

Most users interviewed don't find their profile page useful. It's confusing, infrequently updated, and lacks important info. Users want specific data about their investments directly from the profile. They also want to see personalized data directly from the project page if they have already invested.
