PROJECT

Decentralized Crowdfunding Platform

Background

This project is a decentralised kickstarter where user can back their favorite IP projects via IP tokens or NFTs, while NFT stands for the exclusive content or the licensing for commercial usage right of the content, which is empowered by the "Can't Be Evil" NFT Licensing Framework by A16Z crypto labs.

  • ►
    My Role: ​I am the product designer for the entire website, responsible for related UX/UI Design works and overall Design Standards.

Problem / Challenge

Donate and wait

Compare with traditional crowdfunding platform's workflow, existing user have to lock their fund until the product finally delivered, leads to low liquidity and highly uncertainty for long-term projects

High barrier of trust

NFT investing experience is too risky for those who are not professional investors, and not enough risk control to secure users' fund. Retail investors' confidence are draining out with all the irresponsible conductor in the industry.

Lacking of transparency

There is always an absolute power to refuse, cancel, terminate or delete projects during its crowdfunding, such centralised policy may lead to misconduction or bias from the team which strongly discourages innovation.
